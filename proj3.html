Mission 3: Tracking Harmful Algal Blooms

Protecting Marine Ecosystems

This mission aims to monitor and track harmful algal blooms (HABs) that pose significant threats to marine ecosystems and human health. By observing these events from space, we can enhance our understanding of their patterns and impacts.

Utilizing advanced remote sensing technologies, this mission provides timely data for managing water quality and safeguarding marine resources.

Mission Objectives

  • Identify and map occurrences of harmful algal blooms globally.
  • Analyze environmental conditions conducive to HABs.
  • Provide data for early warning systems to protect public health.
  • Support research on the ecological impacts of algal blooms.

Timeline

  • 2023: Research and development of monitoring technologies.
  • 2024: Pilot studies and validation of data collection methods.
  • 2025: Launch and data collection of algal bloom events.
  • 2026 - 2030: Ongoing monitoring and data analysis.
