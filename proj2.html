Mission 2: Cloud and Aerosol Observation

Understanding Climate Impacts

This mission focuses on observing clouds and aerosols, which play critical roles in the Earth's climate system. By monitoring these components, we aim to improve our understanding of their interactions and impacts on climate change.

Utilizing advanced satellite sensors, this mission provides essential data for climate models and helps assess the effects of aerosols on weather patterns and atmospheric conditions.

Mission Objectives

  • Monitor cloud properties and their effects on solar radiation.
  • Assess aerosol distributions and their climatic effects.
  • Enhance understanding of cloud-aerosol interactions.
  • Support climate change mitigation and adaptation strategies.

Timeline

  • 2023: Instrument calibration and initial observations.
  • 2024: Full deployment and data collection.
  • 2025: Launch of the mission and analysis of collected data.
  • 2026 - 2030: Continuous monitoring and data refinement.
